Recipe Details

Potato Fry

Description: Crispy and spicy fried potatoes perfect for lunch.

Category: Veg

Cuisine: Indian

Meal Type: Lunch

Cooking Time: 10 mins

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Chili powder
  • Turmeric

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into small pieces
  2. Heat oil in a pan
  3. Add potatoes and spices
  4. Fry until crispy
  5. Serve hot
