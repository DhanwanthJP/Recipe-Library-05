Recipe Details

Grilled Gravy

Description: Rich and flavorful grilled gravy with spices.

Category: Veg

Cuisine: Indian

Meal Type: Dinner

Cooking Time: 30 mins

Servings: 3

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Garlic

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add onion and garlic
  3. Add tomato and spices
  4. Cook until thick gravy forms
  5. Serve hot
