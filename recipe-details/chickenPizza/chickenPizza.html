Recipe Details

Chicken Pizza

Description: Cheesy pizza topped with juicy chicken and fresh toppings.

Category: Non-Veg

Cuisine: Italian

Meal Type: Dinner

Cooking Time: 30 mins

Servings: 3

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Capsicum

Cooking Steps

  1. Prepare pizza base
  2. Add sauce and toppings
  3. Sprinkle cheese
  4. Bake in oven
  5. Serve hot
