Recipe Details

Sprouted Salad

Description: Healthy and fresh sprouted salad rich in nutrients.

Category: Veg

Cuisine: Indian

Meal Type: Breakfast

Cooking Time: 10 mins

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Sprouts
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash sprouts properly
  2. Chop vegetables
  3. Mix everything in a bowl
  4. Add lemon juice and salt
  5. Serve fresh
