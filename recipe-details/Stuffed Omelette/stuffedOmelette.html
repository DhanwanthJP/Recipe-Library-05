Stuffed Omelette

Description: Soft and fluffy omelette stuffed with vegetables.

Category: Non-Veg

Cuisine: Indian

Meal Type: Breakfast

Cooking Time: 10 mins

Servings: 1

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Capsicum
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl
  2. Add chopped vegetables
  3. Pour mixture into pan
  4. Cook both sides
  5. Serve hot
