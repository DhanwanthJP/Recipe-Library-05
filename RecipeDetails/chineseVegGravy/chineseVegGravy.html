Recipe Details

Chinese Veggies Gravy

Description: Delicious mixed vegetable gravy with Chinese flavors.

Category: Veg

Cuisine: Chinese

Meal Type: Lunch

Cooking Time: 35 mins

Servings: 3

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Beans
  • Capsicum
  • Soy sauce
  • Corn flour

Cooking Steps

  1. Chop vegetables
  2. Stir fry vegetables
  3. Add sauces and spices
  4. Mix corn flour slurry
  5. Cook until thick gravy forms
