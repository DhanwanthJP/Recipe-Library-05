Recipe Details

Paneer Noodles

Description: Tasty noodles mixed with soft paneer and vegetables.

Category: Veg

Cuisine: Chinese

Meal Type: Dinner

Cooking Time: 15 mins

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Paneer
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Soy sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Fry vegetables
  3. Add paneer cubes
  4. Mix noodles with sauce
  5. Serve hot
