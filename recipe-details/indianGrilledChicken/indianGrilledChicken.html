Indian Grilled Chicken

Description: Spicy and juicy Indian-style grilled chicken with aromatic spices.

Category: Non-Veg

Cuisine: Indian

Meal Type: Dinner

Cooking Time: 40 mins

Servings: 3

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Garlic paste

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt
  2. Let it rest for 30 minutes
  3. Grill the chicken until cooked
  4. Serve hot with sauce
